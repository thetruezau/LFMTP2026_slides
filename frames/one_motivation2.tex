\begin{frame}
  \frametitle{Motivation}

  % The task of
  Mechanizing the metatheory of \textbf{calculi with various syntactic classes} can be challenging, in part due to the binding of variables and its substitution operation.

  \vspace{1ex}
  \onslide<2->{
    For conventional kinds of substitution operations, there exist libraries that automate a significant amount of work (such as Autosubst).
  }
  
  \vspace{1ex}
  \onslide<3->{
    However, examples of \textbf{unconventional substitution operations} appear frequently in the literature (ex: hereditary substitutions, structural substitutions,...).
  }
  
  \vspace{1ex}
  \onslide<4->{
    Our work focuses on formalizing calculi that come from proof theory.
  }

  \vspace{1ex}
  \onslide<5->{
    We want to test the available automation support for such unconventional substitutions found in our case study.
  }
  
\end{frame}
